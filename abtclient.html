What is it

Solar Client is a modification to lunar client. Its made using the great work from the people over at Solar Tweaks. I thought id just take it a little futher and make a client branding out of it.

Solar Client is solar tweaks work combined with lunar's client work and a little bit of my own modification of lunars assets to create Solar Client. if you like what you see and the effort i put into this, consider buying the client launcher and textures. If you dont, all the big stuff ( Not the lc texturepack ) are available for free here

The Future

If motivation comes, I might get in contact with the people behing solar tweaks for their branding and actually create solar client. This is still a possibility, although being 1 person going up against clients with huge development teams, and knowing nothing about client development myself, ill have to see what i can do to get this client actually produced. This is something that i really want to do, but it will probably have to wait untill i get enough resources and knowledge to make a good product.
